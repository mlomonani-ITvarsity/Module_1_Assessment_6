About Yoni Steaming SA

Yoni Steaming SA is a subsidiary or a division of Wellness Clique SA pty ltd and it is located in the heart of Polokwane and in Johannesburg. Our aim is to “heal women using natural products”. Yoni Steaming SA focuses specifically on women and their bodies as a whole. we use holistic methods or medicines to help relieve women from pains that are mostly caused by their lifestyles, things they consume, the environment they live in and other unexplained causes. Our aim is to provide women with natural and un-harmful methods of treating certain conditions. we offer a range of holistic medicines and our products are not made for every woman.
